Photography Services

To create the yearbooks, we use plenty of high-quality photos of your school and students. Consider hiring us to take posed and candid shots of students, as well as photos of school events and activities.

Graphic Design Services

A yearbook requires professional-level graphic design skills to create a cohesive, attractive layout. Consider hiring a graphic designer to create the yearbook cover, design the page layouts, and choose fonts and color schemes.

Printing Services

Once the yearbook has been designed, it needs to be printed. Many printing companies specialize in yearbooks and can provide a range of options, such as paper type, binding options, and printing methods.

Editorial Services

A yearbook should include written content, such as articles and captions. Consider hiring a writer or editor to ensure that the text is clear, concise, and error-free.

Marketing Services

To sell the yearbook, you'll need to market it effectively. Consider hiring a marketing specialist to create a marketing plan, design promotional materials, and manage social media accounts to generate interest and boost sales.

Distribution Services

Once the yearbook has been printed, it needs to be distributed to students and faculty. Consider hiring a distribution company to handle logistics, such as shipping, handling, and storage.

Online Services

Many yearbooks are now available online, either as digital copies or as interactive websites. Consider hiring a web developer to create an online version of your yearbook, complete with searchable content, multimedia features, and the ability to share and comment on photos.
